# Concilium — Sizzle Reel Design

Brand: **Concilium** — "Software by consensus." A council of personas (human + AI
stand-ins) deliberates on every ticket; nothing ships without consensus.
Voice: confident, warm, a little ceremonial — never growth-hacky. Avoid
"supercharge / 10x / copilot / 🚀".

## Mood
Cinematic deep "council chamber" (warm dark ground, single ceremonial rim light)
that **blooms into the warm cream/coral brand palette** at the consensus + lockup
moments. Composed, deliberate, slightly ceremonial.

## Palette
| Token | Hex | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| chamber ground | `#2B221C` → `#160f0a` | deep warm ground (never pure black) |
| cream | `#FCFAF6` / `#F7F1E8` | warm light ground (consensus/lockup) |
| ink | `#2B221C` / `#4B3F35` | dark text on cream |
| **coral** | `#E85D34` (`#F07E5C` light) | **consensus / approval** — the one accent |
| Engineer · Ada | `#1E9C86` (teal) | persona seat |
| Designer · Iris | `#7A57D1` (purple) | persona seat |
| Product Owner · Pam | `#D9962A` (gold) | persona seat |
| QA · Ray | `#2F82C7` (blue) | persona seat |

Persona colors are used ONLY for persona things. Coral is reserved for the
consensus/approval idea. Persona seat order is always Engineer, Designer,
Product Owner, QA.

## Typography
- **Display / headlines:** Fraunces (serif, ceremony) — used for every headline.
- **Body / UI:** Inter.
- **Mono / ticket IDs:** JetBrains Mono.

## Vocabulary (use exactly)
seat · stand-in · the Mediator · council · consensus · verdict · living ticket.
Tagline: **"Software by consensus."** CTA: **"Convene your council."**

## Format
1920×1080, 30fps, ~31s. Music bed + kinetic typography (no spoken VO).
